classmethod convexity_return_mapping(yield_c, yield_d)[source]¶

Perform convexity return-mapping.

For a given set (c, d), the convexity return-mapping works as follows:

  1. If the yield parameters (c, d) lie inside the convexity domain (yield surface is convex), then they are kept unchanged;

  2. If the yield parameters (c, d) lie outside the convexity domain (yield surface is not convex), then they are updated to the convexity domain boundary point along the same angular direction.

Parameters:

  • yield_c (torch.Tensor(0d)) – Yield parameter.

  • yield_d (torch.Tensor(0d)) – Yield parameter.

Returns:

  • is_convex (bool) – If True, then yield surface is convex, False otherwise.

  • yield_c (torch.Tensor(0d)) – Yield parameter.

  • yield_d (torch.Tensor(0d)) – Yield parameter.
